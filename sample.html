VISION & MISSION

Our Collaborative "Trade" model takes direct and fair trade one step further. Coffee is an opportunity to do good, and doing good means we get to support whole communities to make positive change.

Since 2007, Land of a Thousand Hills Coffee has worked with farmers in Rwanda to advance creating a better future through coffee. We build flourishing communities through health initiatives, economic development, and education.
